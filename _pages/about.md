Hi! I am a Ph.D. student at The Ohio State University, advised by Prof. Xueru Zhang. My research interests lie in socially responsible generative AI. Prior to joining OSU, I received my M.S. in Computer Science and Technology from Sun Yat-sen University in 2023, where I worked with Prof. Wuhui Chen on scalable blockchain systems. I received my B.S. in Software Engineering from the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China in 2020.
